About US

Our Sushi & Seafood Resto's attempts to use local, sustainable, and organic ingredients wherever feasible. We work hard to find high-quality ingredients and prepare everything from scratch with care.
We also make every effort to give our employees a livable salary (tips are distributed to all employees, including kitchen staff) and to minimise our environmental impact whenever possible. Overall, these issues result in increased menu pricing, but we hope you discover value and find comfort in knowing that we strive to do better every day at what is essential to us.
